About Our Ministry

Jesus Set Me Free Ministries is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to spreading the Gospel and serving communities in need. We believe in the transformative power of Jesus Christ to set people free from spiritual, emotional, and physical bondage.

Our Vision

To restore and share love as God loved the world, proclaiming His favor and setting the captives free.

Our Mission

Proclamation of the gospel to those who are lost, calling them to repentance and faith in Christ Jesus.
