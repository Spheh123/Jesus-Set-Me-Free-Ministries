Who We Are

Jesus Set Me Free Ministries is a registered non-profit organization (Reg no: 2023/974804/08) dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and serving communities in need. Founded by Apostle Richard Sekgobela, our ministry is built on the foundation of God's Word and the power of the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the transformative power of Jesus Christ to set people free from spiritual, emotional, and physical bondage. Our mission is rooted in John 8:36: "So if the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed."

Our Vision

To restore and share love as God loved the world, proclaiming His favor and setting the captives free.

Our Mission

Proclamation of the gospel to those who are lost, calling them to repentance and faith in Christ Jesus.

Our Core Values

Biblical Foundation

We are committed to the authority of Scripture as the inspired Word of God and the final authority for faith and practice.

Prayer & Dependence

We prioritize prayer as essential to everything we do, recognizing our complete dependence on God.

Compassionate Service

We demonstrate God's love through practical acts of service and compassion to those in need.

Community

We foster authentic relationships and spiritual family, supporting one another in our walk with Christ.

Generosity

We practice generous giving of our time, resources, and talents to advance God's kingdom.

Holy Spirit Empowerment

We rely on the power and guidance of the Holy Spirit in all aspects of ministry and life.

Our Statement of Faith

We Believe in the Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, providing instruction for salvation and Christian living.

We Believe in the Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We Believe in Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension, and personal return in power and glory.

We Believe in Salvation

We believe that for the salvation of lost and sinful people, regeneration by the Holy Spirit is absolutely essential through faith in Jesus Christ.

We Believe in the Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life and to perform spiritual gifts for the edification of the Church.

We Believe in the Church

We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ and in the importance of the local church for worship, service, and fellowship.

Jesus Set Me Free Ministries

Proclaiming freedom, restoring lives, and sharing God's love through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
